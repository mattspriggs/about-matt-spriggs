About Matt

Matt has been a nerd since 1974 when he first discovered pong at a local bar in Winter Park, Colorado after skiing all day with his parents. It was gaming and technology married together that would be his passion for the remainder of his life.

Matt is now entering into the last stage of his working life and making a bold move into the technology sector. A serial entrepreneur, Matt will likely end up working for himself. Thankfully he has other passions besides technology and gaming including:

  • Aikido (Ki-Aikido specifically)
  • Mountain biking
  • Hiking
  • Skiing
  • Hockey and skating
  • Music
  • Art
  • Story telling of all kinds

After years of working in various fields, Matt is FINALLY making a change into a tech field; specifically FULLSTACK WEB DEVELOPMENT. Just to add to the chaos of changing careers, Matt and Sarah (a permanent resident of New Zealand) are moving to New Zealand. Matt has never been to New Zealand but KNOWS that he is ready for something completely different from the Southwestern US where he has lived his entire life!
